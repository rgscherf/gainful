Bulletin Publication

Bulletin publishes twice a week on Tuesday and Thursday (except during holidays).

Summer publication schedule: Delivered on Thursdays only through July and August and resumes a regular schedule after the Labour Day weekend.

Bulletin publication dates for 2015:
Thursday Jan. 8; resumes twice weekly (Tues & Thurs) schedule the week of Jan. 12, 2015.

Bulletin deadlines:
For Tuesday publications – content should be submitted, at the latest, before the preceding Friday.
For Thursday publications – content should be submitted, at the latest, before the preceding Tuesday.

What you need to do:
Send an URL for your event, article or announcement and a brief sentence describing the item before the above deadline to bulletin@utoronto.ca.
