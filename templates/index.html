Gainful: simple, sane public service job postings.

Gainful pulls public service job postings from a variety of sources and presents them to you. No searching for the correct job title, no more folders full of job site bookmarks.

View all current postings, just new postings, just BC postings, just Ontario postings, or just OPS Open Targeted postings.

Gainful updates its job listings hourly.

Visit the about page to see the current list of job sources and learn how you can contribute.
